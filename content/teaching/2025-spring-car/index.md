The "Innovation Practice of Intelligent Car Competition" is a unique interdisciplinary course offered at Tsinghua University, designed to provide students with hands-on experience in the field of intelligent vehicles. This course is a perfect blend of theoretical learning and practical application, allowing students to explore the latest technologies in robotics and autonomous systems.

The course is structured around the annual Intelligent Car Competition, which is organized by the Student Science and Technology Association of the Department of Engineering Physics at Tsinghua University. The competition serves as a platform for students to apply their knowledge in areas such as:

- ROS (Robot Operating System) basics
- Computer vision and audio processing
- Navigation and mapping algorithms
- Control methods for small vehicles and sensors

**Overall, the "Innovation Practice of Intelligent Car Competition" is an excellent opportunity for students to apply their knowledge in a practical setting, fostering innovation, teamwork, and problem-solving skills.**
