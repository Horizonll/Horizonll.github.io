**Welcome to a unique course designed to foster undergraduate innovation by integrating theoretical knowledge with practical applications!**

The field of humanoid soccer robot technology offers a comprehensive platform for innovative practice, and this course is tailored to leverage that potential.

Driven by competition and focused on hands-on learning, this course will equip students with a well-rounded understanding of robot technology through simulation practice and physical demonstrations. Here's an overview of what you can expect from this exciting course:

**Course Objectives**

- **Webots Simulation Platform and ROS Robot Operating System**: Students will learn to utilize the Webots simulation platform and the ROS (Robot Operating System) to gain practical experience in humanoid soccer robot technology. Through experimental learning, we aim to cultivate the ability to participate in the entire research and development process.
- **Physical Demonstration and Simulation Practice**: By working with the "MOS humanoid soccer robot", students will engage in physical demonstrations and simulations to master essential robotics concepts. This includes kinematics, gait planning, stability control, vision, and intelligent decision-making, providing a solid foundation for engaging in scientific research.
- **Competition-Driven Learning**: The course encourages independent exploration and learning through competition. This approach not only stimulates interest in innovative research but also motivates students to push the boundaries of their knowledge and skills.

**Course Content**

- **Introduction to Humanoid Soccer Robot Technology**: Understanding the role of humanoid robots in the context of soccer and the broader implications of this technology in robotics research.
- **Webots and ROS Training**: Hands-on training with industry-standard tools to simulate and program humanoid soccer robots.
- **Experimental Learning**: Conducting experiments to learn about the various components and functionalities of humanoid soccer robots.
- **Robotics Fundamentals**: In-depth study of kinematics, gait planning, stability control, and vision systems.
- **Intelligent Decision-Making**: Exploring algorithms and methods for enabling robots to make autonomous decisions on the soccer field.
- **Competitive Projects**: Participating in competitions to apply learned concepts and encourage innovative problem-solving.

By the end of this course, students will have developed a strong foundation in humanoid soccer robot technology, gained valuable experience in scientific research, and discovered the thrill of innovation through competition. Whether you are a robotics enthusiast or looking to enhance your innovative capabilities, this course is an excellent opportunity to advance your skills and knowledge in a cutting-edge field. Join us and become a part of the future of robotics!
