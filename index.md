I have conducted research on network architecture for foundation models at [LEAP Lab](https://www.leaplab.ai){:target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer"}. Additionally, I served as the Decision Team Leader of the THMOS humanoid robotics team. My research interests are foundation model architecture and Test-Time Training.
